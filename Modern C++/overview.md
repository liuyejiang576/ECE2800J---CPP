1_Review.md is an extension of ECE2800J course content, and introduces some C++11 features.

2_C++11.md is a brief introduction of core C++11 features, with updates from C++14/17/20.

3_STL.md introcudes modern C++ Standard Library, which is easy to understand but powerful in use.

4_Modern.md updates purely new features from C++17/20/23.

There is no fixed order for the 4 parts. You can choose the order that fits your needs. 3 is most beginner-friendly, while 2 is most important, in terms of modern C++ basic thinking.

modern_cpp.md is a concise version of 2,3,4. You can scan it once, and have a general idea of modern C++ features, but concepts are not explained in detail.

modern_cpp_exercises/answers.md are crafted in the ECE2800J-exam style, with which you can check your understanding of modern C++ features.
